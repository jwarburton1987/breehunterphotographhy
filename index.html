Our Mission

Bree Hunter started this business in 2012 in attempt to pursue her lifelong dream of becoming a Professional Photographer and that dream came true when she was able to make it her sole source of income by 2014. She is passionate about Real Estate and Architectural spaces as well as the impact she has been able to make on the sales of homes and assisting Sellers in their journey of moving on to the next chapter in their lives. As a mother of three she understands the importance of capturing a home, not only for the Real Estate Agent to properly market their listing, but to save the memories that were made in the home for the Owners to look back on years later. She strives for perfection and insists on punctuality and professionalism on each individual project.
